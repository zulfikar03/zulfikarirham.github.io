MACHINE LEARNING IMPLEMENTATION FOR SEMICONDUCTOR ENERGY BAND GAP PREDICTION USING GRAPH NEURAL NETWORKS

In this project, we created a machine learning model using Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) to predict the energy band gap of semiconductors. We utilized two specific GNN models for material science: Material Graph Neural Networks (MEGNET) and Crystal Graph Convolutional Neural Networks (CGCNN). This project was developed to overcome the challenges of difficult experimental calculations and the high computational cost of Density Functional Theory (DFT). We further enhanced the prediction accuracy by using transfer learning and ensemble prediction.
